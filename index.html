Fusion 360

Description: Fusion 360 is an all-in-one CAD/CAM/CAE tool that combines 3D modeling with cloud collaboration. Perfect for product design, prototyping, and simulation.

Key Topics:

  • Parametric & Freeform Modeling
  • Simulation & Rendering
  • CAM for CNC Machining
  • Collaboration Tools

AutoCAD

Description: AutoCAD is the industry-standard software for 2D drafting and basic 3D modeling. This course covers engineering drawing creation, dimensioning, layout planning, and architectural schematics.

Key Topics:

  • 2D Drafting & Annotation
  • Layers, Blocks & Dimensions
  • Isometric & Orthographic Views
  • Introduction to 3D Modeling

SolidWorks

Description: SolidWorks is a powerful parametric 3D CAD software used in mechanical design and product development. This course focuses on part modeling, assembly, simulations, and drawing sheets.

Key Topics:

  • 3D Part Modeling
  • Assembly & Mates
  • Motion Study & Animation
  • Engineering Drawing Generation

GD&T

Description: Geometric Dimensioning and Tolerancing (GD&T) is a symbolic language used on engineering drawings to define the allowable variation in part geometry. It ensures design intent, manufacturability, and functional assembly across industries.

Key Topics:

  • Fundamentals of GD&T Symbols
  • Datums & Feature Control Frames
  • Form, Orientation, and Location Tolerances
  • Practical Applications in Engineering Drawings
